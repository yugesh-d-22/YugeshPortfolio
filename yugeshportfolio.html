I'm Yugesh, a multi-disciplinary creative based in Tamilnadu, India. I work at the intersection of graphic design, photography, and digital product design — finding the quiet tension between form and function.

My work is rooted in simplicity — I believe the most powerful designs know what to leave out. Whether I'm building a brand identity, shooting a portrait series, or wireframing a product interface, I bring the same deliberate eye.

Currently open to freelance projects and full-time opportunities. Let's make something that lasts.
